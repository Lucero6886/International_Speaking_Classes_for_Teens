✨ My Personal Style

A speaking lesson about fashion, clothes, and identity. (Ages 13-16 | Level A2-B1)

🎯 Today's Learning Objectives:

  • 👗 Describe clothing items, accessories, and fashion preferences.
  • 🗣️ Express opinions on fashion trends and personal style.
  • 💬 Give polite compliments and advice on outfits.

2. Style Vocabulary 📖

Learn these useful words to talk about your style. Click to see how to use them in a sentence!

Trendy 🌟
(adjective) Modern and very popular right now.
"Oversized sweaters and baggy jeans are very trendy this year."
Casual 🛋️
(adjective) Relaxed and comfortable, not formal.
"I usually wear casual clothes like hoodies and sweatpants on weekends."
Outfit 👕
(noun) A set of clothes worn together.
"She wore a beautiful outfit to the school dance."
Accessory ⌚
(noun) Extra items added to clothes (belts, jewelry, hats).
"A nice watch or a silver necklace is a great accessory."
Vintage 📻
(adjective) High-quality clothing from the past that is stylish again.
"I found an awesome vintage denim jacket at the thrift store."
Second-hand ♻️
(adjective) Not new; previously owned and worn by someone else.
"Buying second-hand clothes is much better for the environment."
Match 🎨
(verb) When colors or clothing items look good together.
"Your white sneakers match your white t-shirt perfectly."
Stand out 👀
(phrasal verb) To be easy to see or notice in a crowd.
"He loves wearing neon colors because he likes to stand out."

3. Speaking Practice 🗣️

Expand the Idea Bank if you get stuck, and read the Sample Answers to see how to structure your speech.

🟢 Easy Questions

Q1. What is your favorite piece of clothing and why? 🧥

💡 Idea Bank
  • A comfortable hoodie / oversized sweater.
  • A pair of favorite sneakers or boots.
  • It is warm, comfortable, or has a cool design.
  • It was a gift from someone special.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

My favorite piece of clothing is my black hoodie because it is very comfortable.

Extended

My absolute favorite piece of clothing is a vintage denim jacket I bought last year. I love it because it matches almost every outfit I wear. Plus, it has lots of pockets which is really useful!

Q2. Do you prefer shopping for clothes in a mall or online? 🛍️

💡 Idea Bank
  • Mall: You can try clothes on, hang out with friends.
  • Online: Faster, more options, sometimes cheaper.
  • I like both, depending on what I need to buy.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

I prefer shopping online because I can easily find my size.

Extended

I definitely prefer going to the mall. Even though online shopping is easier, I hate returning things if they don't fit. At the mall, I can try the clothes on and spend a fun afternoon with my friends.

Q3. What colors do you like to wear the most? 🎨

💡 Idea Bank
  • Dark colors (black, navy) because they match everything.
  • Bright colors (yellow, red) to stand out.
  • Pastel colors (light pink, baby blue) for a soft look.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

I mostly wear black and white because they are easy to match.

Extended

My wardrobe is mostly full of earth tones like dark green, brown, and beige. I think these colors suit my skin tone well. Occasionally, I will wear a bright red accessory to make the outfit pop.

🟡 Medium Questions

Q4. How does your style change in the summer compared to the winter? ☀️❄️

💡 Idea Bank
  • Summer: T-shirts, shorts, bright colors, light fabrics.
  • Winter: Layering, heavy coats, boots, darker colors.
  • My style stays the same, I just add or remove a jacket.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

In winter I wear thick sweaters, but in summer I wear casual t-shirts and shorts.

Extended

My style changes completely depending on the season. In the winter, I love layering clothes, wearing boots, and choosing darker colors. However, during the summer, it gets too hot, so my style becomes very basic with just light cotton t-shirts and bright colors.

Q5. How important are brand names (like Nike, Zara, etc.) to you? 🏷️

💡 Idea Bank
  • Very important: They have better quality and trendy designs.
  • Not important: I care about how it looks, not the label.
  • Important for shoes, but not for regular t-shirts.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

Brand names aren't very important to me. I just buy what looks good.

Extended

To be honest, I think brand names are only important for sneakers. I like to buy famous brands for shoes because they last longer and are more comfortable. But for hoodies or jeans, I usually buy cheaper, unbranded clothes.

Q6. Have you ever bought second-hand or vintage clothes? ♻️

💡 Idea Bank
  • Yes, I go thrift shopping with my friends. You find unique things!
  • No, I prefer buying brand new clothes.
  • I want to try it because it is better for the environment.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

No, I haven't. I usually just buy new clothes from the mall.

Extended

Yes, all the time! I love going to thrift stores. You can find really unique vintage clothes that nobody else has. Also, buying second-hand is much cheaper and it helps reduce waste in the fashion industry.

Q7. Describe an outfit you wore in the past that you now think is embarrassing. 🙈

💡 Idea Bank
  • Wearing clothes that were way too big or too small.
  • A weird color combination (like neon green and bright orange).
  • A t-shirt with a silly cartoon character from when I was younger.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

When I was 10, I wore neon green pants every day. It was terrible!

Extended

A few years ago, I thought it was cool to mix three different patterns. I wore striped pants with a polka-dot shirt. When I look at photos from that time, I feel so embarrassed. It did not match at all!

🔴 Opinion Questions

Q8. Do you think school uniforms are a good or bad idea? Why? 🏫

💡 Idea Bank
  • Good: Everyone looks equal, no stress about choosing outfits in the morning.
  • Bad: Students can't express their personal style, uniforms are often uncomfortable.
  • Compromise: A relaxed dress code is better than a strict uniform.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

I think uniforms are bad because I want to wear my own style to school.

Extended

In my opinion, school uniforms are actually a good idea. Even though they are boring, they save so much time in the morning. You don't have to stress about what to wear, and nobody judges you for not having expensive clothes.

Q9. Some people say fashion is a waste of money. Do you agree? 💸

💡 Idea Bank
  • Agree: Clothes are just for covering the body, spending hundreds of dollars is crazy.
  • Disagree: Fashion is art and a way to express who you are.
  • Middle: It's okay to spend money, but fast fashion is bad for the planet.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

I partly agree. Spending a little is fine, but spending thousands is a waste.

Extended

I completely disagree that fashion is a waste of money. Fashion is a form of art and self-expression. The clothes you wear tell the world who you are. If buying a nice jacket makes someone feel confident and happy, then it is money well spent.

Q10. How do you think fashion and style will change in the next 10 years? 🚀

💡 Idea Bank
  • Technology: Clothes that change color, smart-watches, heated jackets.
  • Sustainability: More recycled materials, fewer fast-fashion brands.
  • Retro: Fashions from the 2000s or 90s will come back again.
📝 Sample Answers
Short

I think clothes will have technology in them, like jackets that heat up.

Extended

I believe fashion will become much more sustainable in the next 10 years. People are realizing that fast fashion is ruining the planet. I think we will see clothes made entirely from recycled plastic, and second-hand shopping will become the normal way to shop.

6. Role-Play Time! 🎭

Work with a partner. Choose your roles and act out the situations. Use the dialogue hints to start!

Scenario A: The Honest Friend 🛍️

Situation: You are shopping at the mall. Student A is trying on a very bright, expensive, and strange-looking jacket. They love it. Student B thinks it looks terrible and is too expensive.

🙋 Student A: Show off the jacket. Explain why you love it and say you want to buy it.

🤷 Student B: Give your honest (but polite) opinion. Suggest they try something else.

💬 Dialogue Hints

A: Wow, look at this! What do you think of this jacket? It really stands out!

B: Hmm, it's definitely unique... but don't you think the neon green is a bit too bright?

A: No way, I think it's trendy! I'm going to buy it.

B: Are you sure? It's really expensive. Why don't we look at...

8. Review Game: Choose the Best Response 🎮

What is the most polite and natural way to reply?

1. Your friend says: "Do you think these yellow pants look good on me?" (You think they look terrible).

2. Your teacher asks: "Why is wearing a school uniform a good thing?"

3. A stranger compliments you: "Wow, I love your vintage jacket! Where did you get it?"
